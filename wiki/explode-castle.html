Slingshot Mechanics

Your primary tool for destruction. The slingshot is used to launch boulders and other special ammo at the enemy fortifications.

  • Aiming

    Click and drag back from the slingshot to aim. The further you drag, the more power is applied to the shot.

  • Trajectory

    A dotted line will appear to show the predicted trajectory of your projectile. Use this to aim for structural weak points.

Ammunition Types

Icon Ammo Type Effects & Interactions
Fireball Creates a large circular explosion upon impact and scatters Fire. Excellent against Wood, Oil, and Gunpowder.
Boulder Heavy and dense. Punches deep into structures, piercing through materials before exploding. Best for breaking Stone and Metal.
Splitter Tap the screen while in flight to split into three smaller projectiles. Great for covering a wide area or hitting multiple weak points.
IceBomb Freezes nearby Sand and Water into brittle Glass upon impact, then shatters them, causing structural collapse.
Lightning Creates a small explosion at the impact point and calls down a massive vertical lightning bolt from the sky, destroying everything in a column.
Tornado Does not explode conventionally. Instead, it creates a vortex that violently scatters loose Debris and Sand outward in a wide ring.
Nuke The ultimate tactical megabomb. Causes a colossal, screen-shaking explosion, spawning fire and scattering debris across the entire map.
Laser Fires a highly concentrated energy beam that instantly pierces a thin line through the entire level structure, destroying anything in its path.
Black Hole Creates a lingering vortex that sucks all loose falling particles (Sand, Water, Debris) towards its center for a few seconds before imploding.
EMP Grenade Instantly disables and deletes all Sci-Fi and Electronic tiles (Forcefields, Nanobots, Batteries, etc.) in a massive radius.
Purifier Releases a cleansing wave that neutralizes bio-hazards (Virus, Poison, Slime, Radioactive), converting them into harmless Water.
Acid Flask Shatters on impact, splattering highly concentrated Acid everywhere. Excellent for melting through thick Metal and Obsidian.
Water Balloon Splashes a massive amount of water. Puts out raging fires, cools Lava into Obsidian, and washes away Mud.
Transmuter A bizarre magical projectile that scrambles all materials it touches into random new materials! Very unpredictable.

Levels

  • Procedural Generation

    The game features an infinite number of procedurally generated levels, with 35 distinct architectural styles (from floating pyramids to twin towers).

  • Win Condition

    To win a level, you must destroy all enemy entities by crushing them under falling debris or hitting them directly with projectiles.
