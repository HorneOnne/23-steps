🍀 Luck System

How luck affects loot drops, chest rewards, and item quantities

What is Luck?

The Luck System is a core game mechanic that influences loot drops from mobs and chests. A higher Luck stat increases both the drop chance and quantity of items, making farming and dungeon exploration more rewarding. Luck can be gained from accessories, set bonuses, potions, and buffs — and can even go negative, penalising your drops!

How Luck Works

✖️
Luck Multiplier
1 + (luck × 0.01)
Base formula that converts your Luck stat into a multiplier. Capped between 0.5× (−50 luck) and 2.0× (+100 luck).
🎲
Drop Chance
base + base × (mult−1) × 0.5
Soft scaling so drops never become guaranteed. A 50% base chance with +50 luck becomes ~62.5%.
📦
Drop Quantity
base × multiplier
Direct multiplier on mob drop amounts. 5 items at +50 luck yields 7–8 items.
🗃️
Chest Item Count
base × (1 + luck×0.003)
Uses 30% of the luck effect to prevent overflow. 8 base items at +50 luck gives 9–10 items.

⚠️
Drop chance uses soft scaling — drops can never become 100% guaranteed regardless of how much Luck you stack. Quantity scaling is direct and scales linearly up to the 2× cap.

What Luck Affects

💀
Mob Loot Drops
All enemy kills
Every item that a mob can drop has its drop chance and quantity scaled by your Luck stat. Higher Luck means mobs drop items more often and in larger amounts.
📦
Chest Loot
All chest types
Chests give you more item slots and higher stack quantities when your Luck is positive. The effect uses 30% of the multiplier to prevent overflowing inventory.
⚡
Stacks with Equipment
Accessories + buffs
Luck from accessories, set bonuses, and potions all add together before the multiplier is calculated. Equip all three lucky items for a combined +75 Luck (1.75× multiplier).
💡
Max Luck Stacking Example: Equip Lucky Charm (+25) + Rabbit's Foot (+35) + Ring of Fortune (+15) and drink a Supreme Luck Potion (+50) = +125 total Luck. After the 2.0× cap this gives you 2× quantity and ~75% drop chance on any 50% base drop.

Planned Luck Features

⚔️
Critical Hit Luck
Combat
Luck may influence critical strike chance — higher luck means a better probability of landing critical hits in combat.
👾
Rare Enemy Spawns
World
With enough luck, rare variants of enemies may spawn more frequently, providing access to unique loot tables not seen on normal mobs.
✨
Enchantment Success Rate
Crafting
Luck is planned to boost the success chance when enchanting items, reducing the likelihood of enchantment failures.
🏆
Quality / Rarity Boost
Loot
A weight-adjustment system (already partially implemented) will allow luck to push rarer item variants to drop more frequently from chests.
