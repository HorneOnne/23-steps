Explode The Castle

Explode The Castle is an exciting physics-based destruction game where you use a slingshot to launch projectiles at enemy fortresses. Load your slingshot, aim carefully, and bring the enemy's fortress crumbling down to crush them.

Master intuitive slingshot mechanics to topple over 5000 unique, procedurally generated levels featuring diverse tile materials like Wood, Stone, Ice, and Sand. Strategize to find the weak points and achieve total destruction.

Features include satisfying physics-based destruction, multiple enemy types, and a variety of challenging tile mechanics.

Available on PC and Web.
