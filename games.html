Tap Miner

Tap to dig, collect resources, and automate your mining empire in this voxel-style idle game.

Tap Miner is a blocky idle mining game. Dig into layers of stone, unlock powerful upgrades, and automate your tools to progress even while offline.

Features

  • Voxel-inspired pixel art
  • Enchanted tool system
  • Offline earnings & automation
  • Upgrade system for pickaxes and workers
  • Multiplayer mining events (coming soon)
Platforms: Android iOS
